1) Proper / Improper / Mixed / Whole

Proper fraction: numerator < denominator (less than 1).
Improper fraction: numerator ≥ denominator (1 or more).
Mixed number: whole number + fraction part.
Whole number: no fraction part (but you can write it as /1).

Proper: 35
Improper: 116
Mixed: 214
Whole: 7 = 71

2) Divide by a Fraction

To divide by a fraction, multiply by the reciprocal.

Example: 6 ÷ 23 = 6 × 32 = 9

Reciprocal of ab is ba (swap top and bottom).

3) Mixed ↔ Improper (convert first!)

You often convert mixed numbers into improper fractions before dividing.

Mixed → Improper: 315 = 165 (because 3×5+1=16)
Improper → Mixed: 143 = 423

4) Division with Proper / Improper / Mixed

Steps:

  1. Convert any mixed numbers to improper fractions.
  2. Change ÷ to × and flip the second fraction (reciprocal).
  3. Multiply across.
  4. Simplify (cancel if possible).
Example: 112 ÷ 34 = 32 × 43 = 126 = 2

5) Three-number Division (left to right)

Division is done left to right: (A ÷ B) ÷ C. Do the first division, then divide by the third number.

Example: 34 ÷ 23 ÷ 2 = ( 34 × 32 ) ÷ 2 = 98 ÷ 2 = 916
